# Step 1 Workflow: Capture Brief And Storyboard

## Objective

Turn the requested video focus into one concrete narrative and storyboard.

## Required actions

1. Ask clarifying questions until the video focus, audience, and single main takeaway are concrete.
2. Read the article and map its arguments, sections, diagrams, or flows to candidate video scenes.
3. Use [scenario-selection-guide.md](scenario-selection-guide.md) to choose the best scenario pattern for the request.
4. Draft the storyboard with [storyboard-template.md](../assets/templates/storyboard-template.md).
5. Keep the runtime within 45-90 seconds unless the user explicitly approves a different target.
6. Stop and ask the user to tighten the brief if the requested scope still spans too many ideas for one strong supporting video.

## Done when

- The narrative focus is explicit.
- The runtime target is explicit.
- The storyboard is concrete enough to implement without improvisation.
