# Step 6 Workflow: Verify Video Integration

## Objective

Verify final playback, embed correctness, and article-page rendering before handoff.

## Required actions

1. Verify the final asset exists in the canonical article media path and the markdown reference is correct.
2. Rebuild the relevant article output and confirm the video renders on the article detail page.
3. Run browser-based playback and visual checks, including a mobile viewport pass.
4. Confirm there are no broken media paths, duplicate embeds, or regressions from the approved preview.

## Done when

- Playback works on the article page.
- Media paths are correct.
- No duplicate embeds remain.
- Verification passes without unresolved regressions.
