# Blog Design Alignment

Keep the finished video aligned to the site's editorial and technical style.

- Favor restrained, text-led scenes over flashy product-demo motion.
- Use typography, spacing, and color choices that feel consistent with the article page rather than inventing a separate brand system.
- Keep diagrams clean, high-contrast, and easy to parse at article width.
- Prefer quiet backgrounds, thin separators, and controlled accents over heavy gradients or novelty textures.
- Let the article's real concepts carry the scene; do not invent decorative metaphors that fight the writing.
- Keep pacing deliberate and credible for technical readers.
