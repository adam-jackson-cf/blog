# Step 4 Workflow: Review Preview And Iterate

## Objective

Collect user feedback on the draft render and iterate until approval.

## Required actions

1. Present the draft MP4 and still frames to the user.
2. Capture requested revisions to story emphasis, pacing, labels, scene order, design alignment, or motion restraint.
3. Update the workspace and re-render previews after each approved change set.
4. Stop and ask for a tighter brief if the requested revisions conflict with the approved narrative, scope, or runtime.
5. Repeat until the user explicitly approves the preview.

## Done when

- The user approves the preview.
- Any scope conflict has been resolved explicitly.
